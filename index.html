About

PSG Institute of Advanced Studies

The PSG &Sons' Charities is one of the oldest and the most vibrant charitable organizations in India. It was formed in the year 1926 by the sons of P.S.Govindasamy Naidu with the committed objectives of promoting education at all levels, without any bias towards gender, caste or creed, and promoting technical education to empower the youth and the deprived sections of the society. PSG Institute of Advanced Studies (PSGIAS) was founded by PSG and Sons' Charities trust to promote high quality research in niche areas of science, technology and management, as well as to create opportunities for PSG Institutions to collaborate with universities abroad.

About

Northern Kentucky University

NKU is a growing metropolitan university of more than 14,000 students served by more than 2,000 faculty and staff on a thriving suburban campus near Cincinnati.

Located in the quiet suburb of Highland Heights, Kentucky—just seven miles southeast of Cincinnati—we have become a leader in Greater Cincinnati and Kentucky by providing a private school education for a fraction of the cost.
